LinkedIn Growth Strategy: Customer Journey Approach

Stage 1: Awareness & Engagement

Goal: Make accounting firms and business owners aware of the problem and generate interactions.

  • Connect with 30-50 people per day (accounting firms, CFOs, Xero users).
  • Follow 10-20 relevant Xero-related businesses.
  • Engage with key posts by commenting valuable insights.
  • Post high-engagement content (polls, thought-provoking questions, comparisons).

Goal: Convert LinkedIn interactions into leads by capturing emails or scheduling consultations.

  • Demonstrate how the solution works using real examples.

Executive Roles & Their Meaning

Understanding executive roles helps target the right decision-makers in LinkedIn outreach.

  • CEO (Chief Executive Officer): The top executive responsible for overall business strategy.
  • CFO (Chief Financial Officer): Manages financial planning, risk assessment, and revenue growth.
  • COO (Chief Operating Officer): Oversees daily operations and internal business processes.
  • CTO (Chief Technology Officer): Leads technology development and digital transformation.
  • CIO (Chief Information Officer): Manages IT strategy and data security.
  • CMO (Chief Marketing Officer): Drives marketing campaigns and brand positioning.
  • CSO (Chief Sales Officer): Responsible for sales growth and revenue strategies.
  • CHRO (Chief Human Resources Officer): Oversees talent acquisition and workplace culture.
  • CISO (Chief Information Security Officer): Ensures cybersecurity and compliance with data regulations.
  • CPO (Chief Product Officer): Focuses on product development and customer experience.
  • CCO (Chief Compliance Officer): Ensures regulatory compliance and legal adherence.
  • CDO (Chief Data Officer): Oversees data management, analytics, and AI integration.
  • CXO (Chief Experience Officer): Enhances customer experience and satisfaction.
